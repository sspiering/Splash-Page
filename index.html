About Me

Graphic Designer | Visual Planner

My style is clean and minimal. I pay high attention to detail. Designing material for print is my strong suit. I find verious degrees of design beautiful, functional and enticing.

I will receive my Bachelor of Arts in Visual Communication Design, June 2016. I am passionate about the work I create as well as being able to collaborate with other. Collaborative work has challenges but also greater reward when it comes to the final out put.

Currently, I work in the Multimedia Center on campus and assist students with various forms of printing and Adobe Software. I am also working in Marketing. From formatting the company handbooks and forms, to workings of day to day small promotions and visual look and feel of the company.
